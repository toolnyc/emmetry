The Modified Register System

In order to place each individual within the family context, the National Genealogical Society developed the “Modified Register System”: each descendant is given a number in the order of their birth within that generation. The superscript number following a name indicates the generation each individual is in their descent from Thomas Addis and Jane Emmet, who constitute the first generation in America. Their children (above) are in generation 2, their grandchildren generation 3, et cetera.

Displaying Names Inline

On generation pages, elements of the Modified Register System are changed as shown below for parents of children in order to facilitate easier reading. Also, the parent who is a member of the Emmet family by birth is displayed first, followed by a link to his or her parents on the previous generation page. If the parent who has married into the family is a woman, her maiden name is shown in parenthesis.
