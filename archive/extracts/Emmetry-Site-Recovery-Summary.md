## Two Eras of the Site

The archive preserves two distinct versions:

**Original static site (2008--2011):** 38 hand-coded HTML pages at www.emmetry.org. These cover the Emmet, LeRoy, Graves, McEvers, and Harris family branches with generation-by-generation pages. Some individuals appear in this version that may not have been migrated to the newer site (e.g., John Duncan Emmet, Marie Louise Emmet, Mary "Minnie" Temple Emmet, Mary "Minnie" Tucker Emmet, Herman Rutgers LeRoy Emmet, Pauline Antonia Berra, Robert (158) Emmet, Thomas (160) Addis Emmet).

**Drupal site (2017--2019):** 263 pages at emmetry.org. This is the fuller, more structured version with individual pages for each person, generation landing pages, and content pages. Each person's page includes structured fields for birth, death, parents, marriages, children, and biographical narrative.

## What This Means for the Rebuild

All the text content, biographical data, genealogical relationships, and images from the original site have been preserved. Nothing appears to be missing from what the Internet Archive captured.

The content is ready to be reorganized and published on a new site whenever you're ready to move forward.
